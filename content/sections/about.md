The Oxford and Cambridge Society of Austria serves past and present members of the Universities of Oxford and Cambridge in Austria. We are committed to providing a vibrant and enriching experience to our members and their guests, fostering a sense of community and intellectual stimulation. _Herzlich willkommen_!​

The Society was founded in 1994 as The Cambridge Society of Austria and re-christened in 2005 as The Oxford &amp; Cambridge Society of Austria. We provide a home for the alumni communities of both universities, proudly furthering the traditional connection between the two universities, as well as a healthy spirit of rivalry, e.g. through our annual screening of the Boat Race. We are a non-profit organisation under the Austrian Verein charter and approved by both universities as the Austrian Alumni Society.
